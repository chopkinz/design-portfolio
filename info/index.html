Industrial Designer

Hey! I'm an Industrial Designer who enjoys tackling challenges through systematic research, concepting, ideation, and testing. Intuitive products with eye-catching details inspire me to push my mind beyond what I've already seen.

My favorite design work wasn't accomplished alone, and I thoroughly appreciate a good team pushing towards a shared goal. Through studio projects and extracurriculars, I've gained the most from learning with others.
